How To Play

Rules of Cat Drop
  • Tap or click inside the glass tumbler to drop your current cat bubble.
  • A rare Golden Ball may appear — it becomes the cat it touches first!
  • A rare Mouse 🐭 may appear — drop her and she scurries down, squeezing between cats and pushing them apart (tail wagging!). Cats can even merge from the shuffle!
  • When two cats of the same level touch, they merge into a larger cat!
  • Classic — normal play with golden & mouse balls.
  • Daily ★ — everyone gets the same spawn sequence today. Compete on the Daily tab!
  • Chaos ↻ — the cup briefly teeters (~22°) now and then, plus sticky, soapy, heavy, explosive & ghost balls.
  • Level 11 (Space Cat) is the ultimate size. Try to reach it for maximum score!
  • Keep cats inside the tumbler. If any cat overflows above the red line for 3 seconds, it is GAME OVER.
  • Use SHAKE (left) to jostle the stack, or POP (needle) to erase one cat.
